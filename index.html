Welcome to Aubie's Helpful Pooch! Come to our on-campus center to rent one of our trained service animals to attend your class or walk around campus with you! We have so many bright and helpful dogs to serve you while you succeed on Auburn's campus.

Service Animal Bios:

Meet Lucky! Lucky comes to us from the Tampa Bay East training facility and can open doors and push wheelchairs among many other helpful skills. Lucky is best-rented for long class times for his patient nature and calm demeanor.

Meet Minnie Mae! Minnie Mae comes to us from the Washington Warrior training facility and can find lost items and load backpacks as well as sniff for lost phones. Minnie Mae is best-rented for long walks across campus with people of limited mobility. She is a very sweet and understanding dog who is observant and in tune with her handler's feelings and surroundings.

Meet Moonbeam! Moonbeam comes to us from a private breeder for students living with disabilities and can take care of you during anytime, especially when you are sick. MoonBeam is specially certified for students with epilepsy.

Meet Lightning! Lightning comes to us from the New York Apple training facility and can cover you with kisses as well as help you with locating your next lecture. Lightning is an expert in Auburn campus navigation and can lead you to your next location as quickly as her name.
